Transport properties in the B phase

Values for he3_tau0, he3_tau_av, he3_diff_* are extended to $T>T_c$ region using normal phase functions.

See also the source file: he3_transp_b.f and doc_tech/spin_diff.pdf.

Collision integral for Bogoliubov quasiparticles:

he3_coll_int(xi, ttc, gap, g0, d0) Full temperature range approximation
{\small (Einzel, Wolfle, Hirschfeld, JLTP80 (1990), p.66)}
he3_coll_int_lt(xi, ttc, gap, g0, d0) Collision integral for low temp (good for $< 0.7 T_c$)
{\small (Einzel, JLTP84 (1991), f.76)}
he3_coll_int_ht(xi, ttc, gap, g0, d0) Collision integral for high temp
{\small (Einzel, JLTP84 (1991), f.75)}

Bogoliubov quasiparticles lifetime and mean free path:

he3_tau0(ttc, p) Lifetime at the Fermi level, s. $\displaystyle\quad 1/\tau(0) = \frac{I (0,T)}{\tau_N(0,T)},$
{\small(Einzel-1991, f.74,76. Einzel-1990 f.A1)}
he3_tau0lt(ttc, p) $1/\tau, T\rightarrow0$ limit (does not depend on energy). {\small(Einzel-1978, f.79)}
he3_tau_av(ttc, p) Thermal average lifetime, s. $\displaystyle\quad 1/\bar\tau = \frac{1}{Y_0} \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \frac{\phi_k\ d\xi}{\tau}$
{\small(Einzel-1991, f.77)}
he3_fpath(ttc, p) Mean free path, cm
{\small (Einzel JLTP32 (1978) f.84)}
he3_tau_dperp(ttc, p)
he3_tau_dpar(ttc, p) Spin diff. transport time, s. \ $\tau_D^{\perp,\parallel} = \bar\tau /(1-\lambda_1^a Y^{\perp,\parallel})$
{\small(Einzel-1991, f.90,96)}

Spin diffusion:

\noindent\begin{tabular}{p{6cm}p{10cm}} he3_diff_hperp_zz(ttc, p)
he3_diff_hpar_zz(ttc, p) Spin diffusion in hydrodynamic limit ($\omega_L \tau \ll 1$), $D^{\perp,\parallel}_{zz}(\omega_L=0)$, cm$^2$/s. According to Einzel's paper (see f.105) nonhydrodynamic effects do not affect $D^\parallel$. In Mukharsky paper they do.
{(\small Einzel JLTP84 (1991) f.102)} he3_diff_perp_xx(ttc, p, nu0)
he3_diff_perp_zz(ttc, p, nu0)
he3_diff_perp_xx_im(ttc, p, nu0)
he3_diff_perp_zz_im(ttc, p, nu0)
he3_diff_par_xx(ttc, p, nu0)
he3_diff_par_zz(ttc, p, nu0)
Components of spin diffusion tenzor, cm$^2$/s
{\small(Bunkov et al, 1990 f.3; Einzel, 1991, f.108;
Markelov, Mukharsky, 1992, f.7,8)}

Problem with $D^\parallel$. According to Einzel-1991 (see f.105) nonhydrodynamic effects do not affect $D^\parallel$ and you should use he3_diff_hpar_zz for any frequency. In Markelov-Mukharsky paper the result for $D^\parallel$ is completely different (it is used in he3_diff_par_xx, _zz). $D^\perp$ is the same in all papers.
